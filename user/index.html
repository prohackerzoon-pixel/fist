FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Can't find your answer? Chat with our team directly using the button below.

💬 Chat With Us →
How do I get a tracking ID? +
Your tracking ID is provided by our admin team once your shipment is registered. It will be shared via our live chat. It starts with NXC-.
How do I pay for my shipment? +
All payment details are discussed directly with our admin team via live chat. Submit your request and we will reach out to discuss pricing personally.
How long does delivery take? +
Express shipments: 2-5 business days internationally. Standard: 7-14 business days. Your admin team will confirm an estimated delivery date when processing your shipment.
What items can I ship? +
We ship electronics, clothing, documents, medical equipment, commercial goods, and personal effects. Prohibited items include weapons and illegal substances. Contact us for specific guidance.
Can I track from any device? +
Absolutely. Your tracking ID works on any device — phone, tablet, laptop or desktop. Simply enter your ID on our tracking page and see the full live timeline.
Do I need an account to track? +
No. Anyone can track a shipment using just the tracking ID on our homepage — no login required. Creating an account lets you place shipments and manage everything from your dashboard.
